# Chicken Noodle Soup
## Ingredients
- 1 whole chicken
- 4 carrots
- 4 celery stalks
- 1 onion
- 4 cloves of garlic
- 6-8 cups broth

### Dumplings
- 1 cup cream of wheat
- &frac12; cup flour
- 3 eggs
- 3 tsp milk or water
