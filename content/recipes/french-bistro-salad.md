# French Bistro Salad
## Indredients
- Green beans
- Raddish
- Hard boiled Eggs
- Dill
- Chives

### Dressing
- &frac14; cups olive oil
- 1 tsp dijon mustard
- Juice from &frac12; lemon
- 1 tsp chopped dill
- 1 tsp chopped chive
- Sald & pepper

## Directions
1. Hard boil eggs

2. Blanch green beans
