# Christmas Sugar Cookies
## Ingredients
- 2 cups flour
- &frac12; tsp baking powder
- &frac14; tsp salt
- 6 tsp butter
- &frac13; cups shortening
- &frac34; cups sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp mills
- 1&frac12; tsp vanilla

### Frosting
- 3 cups powdered sugar, sifted
- 3-4 tsp mills
- 2 tsp light corn syrup
- &frac12; tsp vanilla extract
- gel coloring
- sprinkles (please don't)

## Directions
1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt - set aside

2. Heat butter and shortening for 30sec.

3. Add sugar

4. Beat until fluffy

5. Add egg and vanilla

6. Add dry ingredients

7. Chill for 2+ hours

8. Bake at 375ÂºF for 8-10min
